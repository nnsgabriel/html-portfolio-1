The Best Movies According to Gabriel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

2 Fast 2 Furious

An ex-cop turned street racer gets caught (in his R34 Skyline) and has to help the FBI catch a criminal to be free of his charges. Great stuff.

Avengers: Endgame

Superheroes saving the world doesn't get any better than this.

Madagascar

A wonderful, funny animation about animals fleeing from the zoo to find nature. With some cool penguins.
